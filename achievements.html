Amy Levy PR is a full-service communications agency. Our Los Angeles PR firm has represented clients that range from small startups to multi-million dollar corporations, all of which have one thing in common: a product or service we personally and wholeheartedly believe in.

This nurturing way of doing business produces collaborative relationships unmatched in our industry, which in turn is reflected in everything we do together for your business and customers.

Our services include media relations, social media, advertising, arts publicity, new business launches, reputation management, branding, community relations, media training, strategic planning and trade show/exhibitor consulting. We have recently launched a gluten-free marketing division for our thriving practice, representing products and individuals who serve the Celiac and gluten-intolerant communities.
